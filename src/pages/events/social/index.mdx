## What

Social Days are the mainstay of the MDGC calendar, where most new players get their first experience of organised disc golf.
They are welcoming, friendly rounds where newer players can learn the rules and pick up tips from more experienced players.

A Social Day is usually one round of 18 holes and takes about 2.5 hours.
We typically get 30-60 players, from first-timers through to experienced tournament players.

Groups are usually up to four players.
Event organisers mix cards by skill level so newer players have people around them who can help.
If you have a friend you would like to play with, mention it when you sign up and we will do our best.

## When and where

We split the year into two seasons, usually February-June and July-November, with 15 Social Days in each season.
We alternate between Saturdays and Sundays and rotate around three zones:

- **West Melbourne** - 1st weekend of the month
- **South East Melbourne** - 2nd weekend of the month
- **North East Melbourne** - 3rd weekend of the month

## Registering

Social Days are free for club [members](/club/membership).
Casual players are welcome too: your first round is free, then it is $12 per round after that.

<div class="callout">
**Pre-registration is required** for all Social Days.
</div>

We use [Disc Golf Metrix](https://discgolfmetrix.com/?u=club_events&id=235) to manage registration.
Registration closes on the evening before the event.
Once you have found an upcoming event, click the <span class="metrix-button">Register here</span> button on Metrix.

You will need an [Australian Disc Golf](https://www.australiandiscgolf.com/) number for insurance. It is free and easy to [join](https://www.australiandiscgolf.com/join/).

## Scoring

We also use Metrix to keep scores on the day.
Each group will have at least one experienced player who can help with scoring and using the app.
We prefer to have two people scoring on each card for accuracy.

## Divisions and ratings

Once you have played three rounds, Metrix calculates your rating based on your scores.
Social Days have six divisions based on rating and membership:

- **Div A** - Members with rating above 890
- **Div B** - Members with rating 845 to 890
- **Div C** - Members with rating 790 to 845
- **Div D** - Members with rating below 790
- **Div X** - Members who do not have a rating yet
- **Div Z** - Non-members

## Prizes

Prizes are designed to encourage friendly competition across the whole field.
They are usually MDGC shop vouchers and are awarded for:

- **best handicap score** by division
- landing **closest to the basket** on a designated CTB hole

There is also an optional Ace Club that pays a cash prize to members who get an ace at a Social Day.
