				<div class="results-rule is-prize"><strong>Best net score wins</strong><span>The lowest Metrix handicap score in the division wins.</span></div>

			<h3>CTB Prizes</h3>

				<div class="results-rule"><strong>Latest valid entry</strong><span>The winner is the latest valid CTB form entry for the day and prize group.</span></div>
				<div class="results-rule"><strong>CTB takes priority</strong><span>If a player wins CTB and also leads handicap, the CTB stands and handicap passes to the next eligible player.</span></div>

		<h2>How handicap results work</h2>
		<p>Handicap prizes use Metrix's handicap calculation, not your raw score alone.</p>
	</div>
	<div class="results-split">
		<article class="score-demo-card">

		<div class="results-card-grid">
			<article class="results-card"><span class="results-card-kicker">Raw Score</span><strong class="results-card-title">Throws</strong><p>Your actual total throws for the round. Lower is better.</p></article>
			<article class="results-card"><span class="results-card-kicker">+/-</span><strong class="results-card-title">To par</strong><p>Your raw score compared with course par. A score of -11 means eleven under par.</p></article>
			<article class="results-card"><span class="results-card-kicker">Handicap</span><strong class="results-card-title">Metrix estimate</strong><p>Higher-rated players receive less help; lower-rated players receive more.</p></article>
			<article class="results-card"><span class="results-card-kicker">Net Score</span><strong class="results-card-title">Prize score</strong><p>Raw score minus handicap. This is the number used for handicap prizes.</p></article>
