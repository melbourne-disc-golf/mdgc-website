<Text>

MDGC is committed to providing a safe and welcoming environment for young people at our
events. 

As an affiliate of [Australian Disc Golf](https://australiandiscgolf.com) (ADG), we follow ADG's [safeguarding children and young people policies](https://www.australiandiscgolf.com/keep-everyone-safe/).

## Juniors at events

Juniors taking part in events must complete ADG's [Parental/Guardian Release & Media
Consent Form (ADG-SC-01)](https://www.australiandiscgolf.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Form-ADG%E2%80%91SC%E2%80%9101-V2-%E2%80%93-Parental_Guardian-Release-Media-Consent-Form.pdf).

If you have any questions about playing as, or bringing along, a junior, please
[get in touch](/club#contact-us).

## Working with Children Check

Volunteers in a position of authority over children — for example [Social Day](/events/social)
organisers, tournament directors, and anyone running junior or school events — must hold a valid
Victorian Working with Children Check (WWCC).

- Apply for or renew a check at [Working with Children Check Victoria](https://www.vic.gov.au/working-with-children-check).
- When the check asks for the organisation you volunteer with, use the club's [postal address](/club#contact-us).

## ADG sanctioned events

For ADG sanctioned tournaments, Tournament Directors, Assistant Tournament Directors,
and any other volunteers identified as being in a position of authority over children
must submit their WWCC and SIA Safeguarding Training certificate directly to ADG, at
[scofficer@australiandiscgolf.com](mailto:scofficer@australiandiscgolf.com).
