Melbourne's premier disc golf tournament returns to Bundoora Park for a third year, with an improved 20-hole course now starting from the Golf Course clubhouse! 

### Two days

Melbourne Celebrates runs over two days, with different divisions playing each day. 

- **Day 1** (Sat, 25-Jul-2026): MP40, FP40, MA1, FA1, MA3, FA3, MA50, FA50, MJ15, FJ15
- [Day 2](/events/2026-07-26-melbourne-celebrates-day-2) (Sun, 26-Jul-2026): MPO, FPO, MA2, FA2, MA4, FA4, MA40, FA40

Pick the day that suits you, or play both days for double the challenge and double the fun — with a discount on your 2nd round thrown in.

### Two layouts

- **Advanced Layout** — a pro-level 20-hole course designed to challenge the top divisions and inspire growth in those pushing to play at Australia's highest levels.
  - Divisions: MPO, FPO, MP40, FP40, MA1, MA2, MA40
- **Intermediate Layout** — a 20-hole course better suited to amateur players, decreasing distance without removing the key challenges of holes and allowing different skillsets to shine.
  - Divisions: FA1, FA2, MA3, FA3, MA4, FA4, FA40, MA50, FA50, MJ15, FJ15
