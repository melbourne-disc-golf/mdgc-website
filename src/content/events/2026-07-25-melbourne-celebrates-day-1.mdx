Melbourne's premier disc golf tournament returns to Bundoora Park for a third year, with an improved 20-hole course now starting from the Golf Course clubhouse! 

### Two days

Melbourne Celebrates runs over two days, with different divisions playing each day. 

- **Day 1** (Sat, 25-Jul-2026): MP40, FP40, MA1, FA1, MA3, FA3, MA50, FA50, MJ15, FJ15
- [Day 2](/events/2026-07-26-melbourne-celebrates-day-2) (Sun, 26-Jul-2026): MPO, FPO, MA2, FA2, MA4, FA4, MA40, FA40

Pick the day that suits you, or play both days for double the challenge and double the fun — with a discount on your 2nd round thrown in.

### Two layouts

- **Advanced Layout** — a pro-level 20-hole course designed to challenge the top divisions and inspire growth in those pushing to play at Australia's highest levels.
  - Divisions: MPO, FPO, MP40, FP40, MA1, MA2, MA40
  - [Caddie book](https://udisc.com/courses/bundoora-park-X9BO/layouts/159948/caddie-book)
- **Intermediate Layout** — a 20-hole course better suited to amateur players, decreasing distance without removing the key challenges of holes and allowing different skillsets to shine.
  - Divisions: FA1, FA2, MA3, FA3, MA4, FA4, FA40, MA50, FA50, MJ15, FJ15
  - [Caddie book](https://udisc.com/courses/bundoora-park-X9BO/layouts/160016/caddie-book)

### Schedule

- **8:30am** — Players meeting (shelter near Golf Club Proshop)
- **8:45am** — Round 1
- **12:00pm – 1:00pm** (ish) — Lunch
- **1:15pm** — Round 2
- **4:30pm** — Course pack down
- **5:00pm** — Presentations

### Lunch

A **food truck** will be set up near HQ; you must pre-order (using [this form](https://darebinstadiums.formstack.com/forms/disc_golf_lunch_order_form)) by 11:59pm Wednesday 22 July.

The **Farmhouse Cafe** will also be open, and the **Golf Club Proshop** will have drinks and snacks for sale.

### Parking

Parking is available at the Golf Club lot, the Farmhouse lot, and on the roadside near the holes. Please be mindful of other park users, and carpool to your hole or to the event where possible.

### Water and toilets

- Toilets are at the café and HQ only — none elsewhere on the course.
- Water taps are near HQ and hole 18.

### Refunds

Offered as MDGC vouchers.

- Withdrawals until 7 days prior (before 11:55pm Saturday July 18th): 100% refund.
- Withdrawals between 3 and 7 days prior (before 11:55pm Wednesday July 22nd): 50% refund.

### Notes

- Players who play both days will receive a $10 MDGC voucher _after_ the event.
- All U18 participants must have a supervision approval form completed and submitted to enable them to play this event. This form must be submitted to the TD at least 24 hours prior to the event start time to enable groups to be established with parents or approved adults providing supervision.
