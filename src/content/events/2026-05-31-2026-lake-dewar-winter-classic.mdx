A one-day disc golf tournament set in the scenic surroundings of Lake Dewar Discovery Camp, Victoria.

- **Format:** 2 rounds of 20 holes (same day).
- **Capacity:** 50 (we'll have 5-person cards to allow more folks to play)
- **TD:** Carlton Klerck
- **Asst TD:** Daniel Santamaria

### Schedule

- Player meeting: 8:30 AM
- Round 1: 8:45 AM
- (BBQ lunch provided)
- Round 2: 1:15 PM

### Entry includes

- Tournament entry
- BBQ lunch, soft drinks, and water
- Prizes and awards
