We're excited to host this **women-only** event as part of the [PDGA Women's Global Event](https://www.pdga.com/women/global-event), 
and to celebrate [50 Years of Flying Disc Sports in Australia](https://www.facebook.com/61588565020379/posts/50-years-of-flying-disc-sports-in-australia2026-marks-the-50th-anniversary-of-fl/122102310915285500/).

Join us on **Sunday 17 May** at **Ruffey Lake Disc Golf Course** for a day of fun, connection, and great throws. 

The day will include **two rounds of disc golf**, with **lunch provided** in between, plus **two scheduled coaching sessions**.
It's a great opportunity to meet other women in the community, learn new skills, and be part of a global celebration of women in disc golf.
We'd love to see you there. Come along, bring a friend, and enjoy a relaxed and supportive day on the course.

Perfect for (female) players of all levels. Entry fee is **$20**.
