Mando Madness returns - it's Bald Hill, but not as you know it. A brand-new layout, with more mandos than you can shank a disc at.

As in previous years, the day will consist of two rounds - doubles in the morning, and singles in the afternoon - with a BBQ lunch provided in between. You can play either or both, no need to commit to the whole day. For the doubles, just sign up individually and we'll do a seeded draw to decide teams.

This is a free event, open to MDGC members. Sign up for doubles here:
