Come on down to the **third instalment of the Ruffey Rumble**. Challenge yourself on the expanded full 18 hole layout at Ruffey Lake Park.

The event will be 2 rounds in a day with divisions split over the 2 days.

* **Saturday, Jan 31st**: MPO, FPO, MA2, FA2, MA4, FA4, MA40, FA40
* **Sunday, Feb 1st**: MP40, FP40, MA1, FA1, MA3, FA3, MA50, FA50, MJ15, FJ15

Pick the day that suits you or double up for double the challenge and double the fun.

Part of the **MDGC Summer Cup** series.
In addition to the standard Summer Cup features there will be prizes and trophies for the individual events like the previous 2 years.
