Melbourne’s premier disc golf tournament returns to Bundoora Park for a third year, with an improved 20-hole course now starting from the Golf Course clubhouse! Melbourne Celebrates will comprise two days, with different divisions playing each day. Pick the day that suits you or play both days for double the challenge and double the fun, with a discount on your 2nd round thrown in!
  
**Divisions**

Saturday, July 25th: MP40, FP40, MA1, FA1, MA3, FA3, MA50, FA50, MJ15, FJ15

Sunday, July 26th: MPO, FPO, MA2, FA2, MA4, FA4, MA40, FA40
 
**Layouts**

Advanced Layout: A pro-level 20-hole course designed to challenge the top divisions and inspire growth in those pushing to play at Australia’s highest levels.

Divisions playing Advanced Layout: MPO, FPO, MP40, FP40, MA1, MA2, MA40
 
Intermediate Layout: A 20-hole course better suited to amateur players, decreasing distance without removing the key challenges of holes and allowing different skillsets to shine.

Divisions playing Intermediate Layout: FA1, FA2, MA3, FA3, MA4, FA4, FA40, MA50, FA50, MJ15, FJ15
 
**Rough Schedule (TBC closer to the event)**

Lunch: \~12pm - 1pm

Round 2 Commences: 1:15pm

Course pack down: 4:30pm

Presentations: 5pm
 
**Refunds**

Offered as MDGC vouchers.

Withdrawals until 7 days prior to the event will receive a 100% refund (before 11:55pm Saturday July 18th).

Withdrawals between 3 and 7 days prior to the event will receive a 50% refund (before 11:55pm Wednesday July 22nd).
 
\* Discounts will be given to those who played both days, as a $10 MDGC voucher sent _after_ the event.
