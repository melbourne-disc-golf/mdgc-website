Bundoora Park is the home of Melbourne Celebrates, the club's premier tournament. 

A temporary 20-hole course is set out over the parkland, with advanced and intermediate layouts to suit different divisions.

## Facilities

- parking, toilets, food and coffee at the Bundoora Park Cafe
