Bundoora Park is the home of Melbourne Celebrates, the club's premier tournament. It is a temporary course, set up in Bundoora Park for the event rather than a permanent installation.

The 20-hole course is set out over the parkland, with advanced and intermediate layouts to suit different divisions.

## Facilities

- parking, toilets, food and coffee at the Bundoora Park Cafe

## Getting there

The course is set up in Bundoora Park, off Plenty Road in Bundoora.
