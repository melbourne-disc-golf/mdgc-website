**Barwon Valley Fun Park** is home to Geelong's first public disc golf course, installed in late 2013 on the banks of the Barwon River, running right through the heart of Geelong.

The park is typically well utilised by families, dog walkers, and various other river users. Originally installed with 9 baskets and 9 tees (the front 9), the course has since been updated with 9 additional tees (the back 9), combining for 18 unique holes.

## Features

- 9 baskets, 18 tees (two layouts)
- DGA Mach III baskets
- Concrete teepads
- Free to play

## Facilities

- Parking nearby
- Picnic areas
- Beautiful riverside setting
