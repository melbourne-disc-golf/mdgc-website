Located near Melbourne CBD under the iconic Westgate Bridge, **Stony Creek** is very popular with folks who live or work in the inner-west.

The course dates back to 2017, when it had with 3 permanent baskets and 1 homemade basket.
Since then, it has undergone a major upgrade to expand to the current 9-basket layout with concrete teepads.

## Features

- 9 holes

## Getting there

### By car

- Parking is available near the course at 310 Hyde Street, Yarraville.
- More parking is available in the neighboring area, including: Hughee, Lormer, Beverley Streets.

### By public transport

- **Train** (**Yarraville** Station) - about 1.5 kms away
