The **Inverleigh Golf Club** is home to Victoria's first Championship level disc golf course, located just 20-30 minutes drive from Geelong.

The facility offers a Championship level 18 hole course and a smaller 9 hole beginner course. A good mix of heavily wooded, tight technical holes mixed with long open golf fairways, this course has a variety of challenges for disc golfers of all skill levels.

Come play a round with the kangaroos at Inverleigh!

## Features

- 18 holes (championship course)
- 9 holes (beginner course)
- Par 64, 2920m
- RAD Ace baskets
- Synthetic turf teepads

## Cost

This is a pay-to-play course; contact the [golf club](http://inverleighgolfclub.asn.au/) for current rates.
