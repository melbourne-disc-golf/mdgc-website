**Bald Hill** is located in Clarinda, in south-east Melbourne.

Designed specifically to be beginner-friendly, this collaboration between the MDGC, the City of Kingston, and course designers [RAD](https://rad-creations.com/disc-golf/), has resulted in an exciting course that's great for players of all skill levels.

## Features

- 18 holes (18 tees, 9 baskets)
- full course signage
- practice basket area with "how to play" sign for new players
